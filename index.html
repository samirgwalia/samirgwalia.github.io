Behind the art


As I remember it was rainy midnight of winter, and I was sitting at a bench of my college park with my mixed thoughts. Emotions were moving too and fro like the waves of the ocean. My mind was unable to process anything, and suddenly one thought stuck in my mind that if love is the purest creation of god then why it is so complicated. I kept thinking about it and just noted down all the thoughts in my phone's notes in form of a poem, knowingly-unknowingly it was in rhythm. I was listening to "Mere Saiyaan" on loop those days it is a sweet composition. So I forwarded my poem to Shourya Kumar Lal (the composer of Mere Saiyaan). He introduced me to Aarvi sir, he liked it but it was in the format of a poem so he suggested I work on it again according to a rhythm. I was a newbie so it was a better option to collaborate with Surya Samudra (today we are friends). And unfortunately, my phone got damaged and I was offline for more than 2 months. I then received a call from Aarvi sir that song is ready in the voice of Shahid Mallya sir and I was at cloud 9, I became so happy and the adrenaline was sprinting in my veins. Soon the video got recorded featuring Manjul Khattar and Rits Badiani. This was enough to flex among my friends. and finally, after a journey of almost a year song got released in January 2020.
